Our History

Our story begins in 2004, when two brave women, just like Lampião's wife, founded this restaurant. They overcame barriers, chasing the goal of always bringing products of the best quality.

In 2009, 5 years after its founding, Maria Bonita gained national and international renown, graced by the presence of celebrities such as Hebe Camargo, Ana Maria Braga, Fátima Bernardes, and many others.

However, it was in 2012, amidst the buzz of the economic crisis, that we faced the biggest challenge in our history, where we almost had to close the restaurant; but with the help of renowned chefs, we managed to overcome this obstacle.
